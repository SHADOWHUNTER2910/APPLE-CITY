Simple Format

Format: Product Name, Cost Price, Selling Price, Quantity, Expiry Date

Examples:

  • With Expiry: Paracetamol 500mg, 3.50, 5.50, 500, 2027-12-31
  • With Expiry: Amoxicillin 250mg, 8.00, 12.50, 200, 2027-10-31
  • No Expiry: Bandages Pack, 5.00, 8.50, 250,

Notes:

  • Only 5 fields needed - super simple!
  • SKUs and batch numbers are auto-generated
  • Cost Price: your purchase/cost price (for profit tracking)
  • Selling Price: the price you sell to customers
  • Expiry Date: leave empty if product doesn't expire, format YYYY-MM-DD
  • Manufacturing date is automatically set to today
